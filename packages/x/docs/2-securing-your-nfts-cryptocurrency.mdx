# Securing your NFTs/Cryptocurrency

Currently X Marketplace only supports Metamask. Built by ConsenSys,
Metamask is one of the most widely adopted cryptocurrency wallets used today to interact with decentralised applications.

- [Getting Started with Metamask](https://metamask.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/sections/360002198491-Getting-Started)

- [Privacy and Security](https://metamask.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/sections/360012783592-Privacy-and-Security)

- [Managing your tokens and how to add new ones](https://metamask.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/sections/360012899951-Managing-my-Tokens)

- [General FAQ](https://metamask.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/sections/360002148511-FAQ)

- [Everything else you should know](https://metamask.zendesk.com/hc/en-us)

- [Add Fantom network to your Metamask](https://docs.fantom.foundation/tutorials/set-up-metamask)

- [Add Binance Smart Chain to your Metamask](https://academy.binance.com/en/articles/connecting-metamask-to-binance-smart-chain)

Although it is not required, it is strongly recommended that users use a hardware wallet such as [Ledger](https://www.ledger.com/).
This works in conjunction with Metamask to more safely secure your cryptocurrency and NFTs.
